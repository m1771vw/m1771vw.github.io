One Second

One Second is an iPhone app where players press a button to start a timer. If they are able to hit one second on the dot, then they get a point. Their score keeps rising if they hit it consistently, but when they miss, their score resets.

Technology Stack

Swift, iOS

What Was The Game?

One Second is an iPhone app where players press a button to start a timer. If they are able to hit one second on the dot, then they get a point. Their score keeps rising if they hit it consistently, but when they miss, their score resets.

History Behind The Game

I had thought about making a game that was "easy to play", seem "easy to get points", but also "hard to win."
