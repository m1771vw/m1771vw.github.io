UAV Forge

This is a side project at the University of California, Irvine.

Technology Stack

C++, QTCreator

What Was The Application?

This application's purpose is to operate an Unmanned Aerial Vehicle.

History Behind The Game

The project was first started as a senior project, and was continued into the following year. The project leads reached out to ICS and Engineering students for a chance on collaborating on a big project together.

Problems, Mistakes, and Lessons

Within the GUI team, there weren't many issues - we got along well, and we were able to attend most of our scheduled meetings.
